### **1. CLIENT CONSULTATION MODE**
```
WORKFLOW:
1. Stylist opens app, creates new consultation
2. Client sits in chair, stylist positions iPad camera
3. Real-time AR shows hair color options
4. Swipe through 400+ professional colors
5. Client picks favorite
6. App generates exact formula with:
   - Brand & product line
   - Color codes
   - Developer volume
   - Mixing ratios (grams)
   - Processing time
   - Application technique
7. Save consultation to client profile
8. Email before/after preview to client
```
